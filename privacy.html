SetX Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

SetX is a card game that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app functions entirely offline and does not require any internet connection.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or usage statistics. The app is designed to be played locally on your iPad without any need for internet connectivity or data storage beyond the device.

App Functionality

SetX is a multiplayer card game designed to be played on a single iPad. All game data is temporary and exists only during active gameplay sessions.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the app, please contact us via GitHub at https://github.com/benjosb/SetX
