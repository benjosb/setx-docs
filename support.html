SetX Support

How to Play

  • Single Player: Use practice mode to learn the game at your own pace
  • Multiplayer: Place the iPad flat on the table and gather around with 2-4 players
  • Getting Started: Choose your avatars and follow the interactive tutorial

Game Rules

  • Find SETs of three cards where each feature is either all the same or all different
  • Features: number (1, 2, 3), color (red, green, purple), shape (oval, diamond, squiggle), and shading (solid, striped, open)
  • Advanced players can try finding SuperSETs for extra points!

Technical Requirements

  • Compatible with iPad
  • Requires iOS 15.0 or later
  • Works completely offline - no internet required
